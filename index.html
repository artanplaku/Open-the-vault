Save Them Tutorial

Welcome to Save Them Tutorial. This is a two player game.

Player One: Your job is to write a word that represents the vault password.

Your word should follow these guidelines:

  • Keep it at one word
  • Only do letters, no numbers

    • Player Two: Your job is to guess the word that Player One wrote. Keep in mind that you have 1 min to guess the word and you can make up to 5 mistakes. If you find the word correctly the vault will open and you will have won the game.

    If you guess the wrong letter, you will see that letter appear in the wrong letters box. Once you have made 5 mistakes the game will be over.

    The game is also over if the timer goes to 0.
